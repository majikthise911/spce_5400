- CRYPTO-SAT (novelty 3.5/5, validation 2/5) = 59.5 (below threshold)
- PLASMA-SCOUT (novelty 3/5, validation 5/5) = 84.5 (excellent)

- RPO-TARGET and QKD-LEO had decent scores (73.5, 71.5)

- **Takeaway:** Single weak dimension can disqualify entire mission

### Lesson 3: Domain Alignment Matters
- PLASMA-SCOUT perfect fit (Space Weather = your #1)
- Adds 15 points from domain alignment alone
- **Takeaway:** Pick missions that excite you personally

### Lesson 4: CSLI Score ‚â† Success
